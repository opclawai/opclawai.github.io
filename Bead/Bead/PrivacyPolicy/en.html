How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing Core Services — Process your uploaded photos to generate bead patterns and BOM lists
  • Improving the App — Analyze usage patterns to enhance features and user experience
  • Bug Fixing — Diagnose and resolve technical issues based on crash logs and error reports
  • Customer Support — Respond to your inquiries, feedback, or feature requests
  • Legal Compliance — Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal obligations

Your photos and generated content are processed primarily on-device. We do not store your uploaded photos on our servers unless you explicitly choose to share them for support purposes.

Data Storage & Security

3.1 Storage Location

Your photos, generated bead patterns, and BOM data are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store this content to our cloud servers during normal usage. Analytics data may be processed through trusted third-party services that comply with industry-standard security practices.

3.2 Security Measures

We implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect your information, including encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL) and industry-standard access controls. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

3.3 Data Retention

Your locally stored data (photos, patterns, BOMs) remain on your device until you delete them or uninstall the App. Analytics data is retained for a period reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, typically no longer than 24 months.

4.4 With Your Consent

We may share your information for other purposes with your explicit consent.
