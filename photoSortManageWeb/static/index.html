常见问题FAQ

关于相册权限、删除恢复、隐私保护区和广告的说明。 Notes about photo permission, restore, privacy vault, and ads.

PhotoSort 会上传我的照片或视频吗？Does PhotoSort upload my photos or videos?

核心整理功能以本地处理为主。我们不运营自有云端相册，也不会把你的照片、视频、隐私保护区文件或截图文案上传到我们自己的服务器。Core cleanup is local-first. We do not operate our own cloud album, and we do not upload your photos, videos, vault files, or screenshot notes to servers operated by us.

删除后还能恢复吗？Can I restore deleted items?

通过 PhotoSort 删除的项目会先进入应用的 7 天回收站。你可以在回收站中恢复，或选择彻底删除来释放空间。Items deleted through PhotoSort enter the app's 7-day trash first. You can restore them there or permanently delete them to free space.

为什么需要相册权限？Why is photo library permission needed?

相册权限用于读取本机照片和视频、统计容量、筛选截图和 Live Photo、生成静图、保存恢复内容，以及把隐私保护区导入后的原始项目从系统相册移除。Photo library permission is used to read local photos and videos, calculate storage, find screenshots and Live Photos, create still images, save restored items, and remove originals after vault import.

应用会展示广告吗？Does the app show ads?

当前项目已接入 Google AdMob，用于开屏、横幅和插页广告。广告相关数据处理以 Google 的规则和本隐私政策说明为准。The current project integrates Google AdMob for app open, banner, and interstitial ads. Ad-related processing follows Google's rules and this privacy policy.
