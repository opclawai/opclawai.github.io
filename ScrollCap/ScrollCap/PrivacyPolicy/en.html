Introduction

Welcome to ScrollCap. We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal data. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Notice: ScrollCap is a scrolling long screenshot tool. We do NOT collect any of your screenshot content or operation data.

1. Information Collection and Use

We collect information only to provide and improve our services:

  1. Device Information: We may collect basic device info such as model, OS version, and device ID to ensure app stability and security. This data cannot identify you personally.
  2. Screenshot Function: All screenshot processing happens locally on your device. We do NOT record, upload, or store your screenshots, screen content, or operations.
  3. Local Data: All settings and edited images are saved only on your device. We have no access to them.

2. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We do not use cookies to track or collect personal information. Any temporary data is only used for app functionality and remains on your device.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure
