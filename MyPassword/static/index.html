常见问题FAQ

上架前后，用户最关心的安全和迁移问题。The safety and migration questions people usually ask first.

忘记主密码怎么办？What if I forget the master password?

为了保护本地数据，我们无法替你找回主密码。请牢记主密码，并在更换设备或卸载前完成备份。We cannot recover it for you. This protects local data, but it also means you should remember your master password and keep backups.

备份还原会上传服务器吗？Does backup upload data to a server?

不会。二维码备份还原由你主动触发，用于设备间迁移。请在可信环境中展示二维码。No. QR backup and restore are user-initiated for device-to-device migration. Show migration QR codes only in trusted environments.

应用是否包含广告？Does the app include ads?

应用可能展示由 Google AdMob 提供的广告。广告不会改变保险库数据本地保存的原则。The app may show ads provided by Google AdMob. Ads do not change the local-first design of your vault content.
